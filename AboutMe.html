Temperature controlled oscillating fan using arduino (C++ framework)

This is a fan duct taped to a servo motor

How it works: The program gets the air temperature from a thermistor. If the air temperature is greater than 75¬∞F, the arduino will provide HIGH voltage to the servo motor and fan. The servo motor rotates as the fan blows cool air.

Hardware project

A 'make you get out of bed' alarm clock

A 'make you get out of bed' alarm clock

This is a frequency changing alarm clock that only turns off when you get out of bed since the 'off' button is located 12 feet away from the alarm.

How it works: There is an ardiuno function called millis which counts milliseconds. I converted that function to seconds, and then displayed the elapsed time on a liquid crystal display. You can input at what time you want the alarm to go off. The alarm is programmed to change frequencies, so as the alarm goes off for longer, it gets more annoying. The 'off' button is located 12 feet away to force you to get out of bed. The button resets the circuit and turns off the alarm.
